Who We Are

Born from a Passion
for Coffee

Caff Own Coffee started with one simple dream — to create a space in Jaipur where people could slow down, savour a great cup, and feel at home. Located in the heart of Jagatpura, we've built more than just a café. We've built a community.

From students to professionals, breakfast dates to late-night study sessions — our doors are open for everyone. With free Wi-Fi, a cozy ambiance, and a menu that keeps you coming back, Caff Own Coffee is your second home in Jaipur.

We're proudly 100% vegetarian, sourcing fresh ingredients and crafting every dish and drink with care. That's why our customers keep returning — and keep telling their friends.
